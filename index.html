Hello, have you ever wanted to watch Star Wars in the exact order it takes place? You've come to the right spot, this is a website that gives you all the Star Wars TV shows and movies in chronological order. Be warned some episodes of some of the shows take place before other things (see full list below)
Some helpful info I wish I knew when I started looking at the order of everything:
BBY
